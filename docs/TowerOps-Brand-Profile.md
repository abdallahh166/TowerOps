# TowerOps Brand Profile

## Brand Identity
- Product name: `TowerOps`
- Company: `Seven Pictures`
- Product type: telecom field-operations platform for subcontractors

## Target Market
- Primary buyers: telecom maintenance subcontractor companies
- Primary users:
  - Admin
  - Office Manager / Supervisor
  - Field Engineer
  - Client portal users (tower owner/operator side)

## Business Positioning
TowerOps helps subcontractors that execute **Preventive Maintenance (PM)** and **Corrective Maintenance (CM)** on telecom sites owned by operators or tower companies.

Examples of end-clients served by subcontractors:
- Orange
- Vodafone
- WE
- IHS

## Core Value Proposition
- Standardize PM/CM workflows end-to-end
- Enforce SLA and evidence quality
- Improve assignment, execution, and review cycle time
- Provide auditable reporting and client-facing visibility

## Naming Policy
- External/product-facing name: `TowerOps`
- Internal technical names are now aligned to `TowerOps` across solution, projects, namespaces, and documentation.

## Suggested Tagline
`TowerOps - PM/CM Operations Control for Telecom Subcontractors`
